Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Aevora handles personal information and device-related data when you use our website, products, and services.

Depending on the product or service, we may collect contact details, support communications, technical logs, crash diagnostics, and usage events needed to operate and improve reliability.

How We Use Information

We use collected data to deliver requested functionality, maintain service quality, troubleshoot issues, support release readiness, protect security, and communicate important updates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with infrastructure, analytics, monitoring, or support providers only when required to operate and secure services.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary for operational, legal, and security purposes. Retention periods vary by data type and service requirements.

Your Choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold, subject to applicable legal and operational obligations.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
